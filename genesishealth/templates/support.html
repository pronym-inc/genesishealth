Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my Genesis Blood Glucose Monitor?

You can activate your Monitor at the pharmacy in which you received your meter or by calling Genesis Health Technologies at 888-263 0003.

How do I set up my MyGHR personal account?

You will be able to set up your MyGHR online logbook when you activate your monitor at the pharmacy in which you received your meter or by calling Genesis Health Technologies at 888-263 0003.

How do I send a text message alert of my test results to a cell phone?

A text message alert of each reading can be sent to a cell phone by clicking on the Alerts Tab inside your MyGHR system and adding the cell phone number or email address you wish the alert to be sent to.

How do I set my personal highs and lows into the MyGHR System?

You can set your high and low ranges by clicking on the Health Information tab in your MyGHR system and inserting the information for both high and low levels. The MyGHR system will automatically set a default range of 90-120.

How do I print out copies of my reports?

At the bottom of each report, click the “Print” button.

How do I recover my username & password?

You can reset your username and password online at www.genesishealthtechnologies.com or by calling Genesis customer support at 888 263 0003.

What if my test results do not send to my MyGHR account?

Your reading will store on your meter and be sent the next time you test.

How do I add notes to my personal MyGHR logbook?

Move over the specific reading you want to add notes to and right click on your mouse.
